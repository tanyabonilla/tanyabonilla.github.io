Operationalization

We can use the data that we have collected and models we have created to inform consumers of what places in New York are cheaper to look at when considering staying in New York. This could cause potential consumers to be more economical in their trips and be willing to use Airbnb more.

Social Impact & Consequences

Some consequences that could come from informing consumers about such findings though are that some hosts on Airbnb could lose revenue due to the change in consumer wants. Say their place is nice menaing their price is higher, then there is a chance a potential client could look elsewhere. Another consequence would be that small-business motels/inns or B8Bs lose clientele as well.
